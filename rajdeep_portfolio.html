How it works

Simple & transparent.

A clear workflow so there are never any surprises — just great work.

01

Brief & Discovery

We talk through your vision, reference clips, and timeline. I ask the right questions so the edit feels right from the first cut.

02

Proposal & Pricing

You get a clear quote with scope, deliverables, and turnaround time. No guesswork, no hidden extras.

03

First Cut

The first assembly lands in your inbox via a private review link — usually within 48 hours of receiving footage.

04

Revisions

Two rounds of revisions included. We refine together until every frame is exactly where it should be.

05

Delivery

Master files in your preferred format — H.264, ProRes, or platform-ready specs. All versions, all aspect ratios.
